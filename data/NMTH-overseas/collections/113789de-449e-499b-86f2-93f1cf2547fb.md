## Description

史蒂瑞博士（Dr. Steere）在福爾摩沙發現了藪眉屬（Liocichla），命名為黃胸藪眉（Liocichla steerii），寄給史溫侯確認。

## 英文 (ORIGINAL)

##--SPLIT--##
XLI.—On a new Bird from Formosa.
By R. Swinhoe, F. R. S. &c.
(Plate XIV.)
Dr. Swinhoe, whose ornithological discoveries in the Philippines have lately attracted so much attention, also visited Formosa during his travels in the east. The portion of the island traversed by him lay towards the southern extremity, where he penetrated into the mountains of the interior, not visited by me. Amongst several interesting species obtained by him and submitted to me, such as Suthora bulomachus, Sibia auricularis, Garrulax taivanus, &c., was a Liothrix-like bird, which is quite new to me. Wishing for further information, I waited until I had an opportunity of showing the specimen to Lord Tweeddale. It was new also to him; and he writes, “It is another evidence of the close connextion that must have existed formerly between Formosa and the Himalayan chain.” I will therefore bring forward this species under the generic name.
LIOCICHLA, gen. nov.
In general characters a Liothrix, but with the stronger legs and shorter wings of a Garrulax, and somewhat allied to Sibia.

##--SPLIT--##
LIOCICHLA STEERII, sp. nov. (Plate XIV.)
Olivaceous green throughout; crown and occiput, chin and throat, flanks and rump smoky; patch of orange-yellow on anterior corner of eye; streaks of yellow on sides of the nape and vent-feathers, broadly tipped with orange; breast, belly, and edge of wing yellow; axillaries smoky; bill and legs light wood-brown; tail olive-green, feathers square at the ends and white, four central rectrices with a black bar before the white tips, three on each side with the apical portion of outer web black as well; secondaries washed with maroon, black on inner webs and apical half, all tipped with white, yellowish green on outer webs, stems black, Length 7.5, wing 2.7, tail 3.4.

## 中文 (TRANSLATION)

##--SPLIT--##
XLI─論福爾摩沙的一個新鳥種。作者羅伯特‧史溫侯，皇家科學學會院士
圖14
史蒂瑞博士（Dr. Steere）1最近在菲律賓群島上的鳥類學發現備受世人矚目，他在東方旅行期間，也來到了福爾摩沙島。他所遊歷的那個地區位於福爾摩沙島最南端，深入到了我未曾進入過的內山。他寄了幾件他採集到的有趣鳥種給我，例如粉紅鸚嘴（Suthora bulomachus，目前學名 Sinosuthora webbiana）、白耳畫眉（Sibia auricularis，目前學名 Heterophasia auricularis）、臺灣畫眉（Garrulax taivanus，目前學名 Garrulax taewanus）；還有一種對我來說也相當新奇，類似相思鳥屬（Liothrix，目前屬名Leiothrix）的鳥類。我一直等著向推代爾爵士（Lord Tweeddale）展示本標本，希望可以獲得更多資訊。推代爾爵士也沒看過這種鳥，他在信中寫道：「這是另一件證據，證明了以前福爾摩沙島和中國喜馬拉雅之間必定存在著緊密的關係。」我將用以下屬名，介紹這種新鳥種。
藪眉屬（Liocichla），新屬。
整體來說，其特徵類似於相思鳥屬，但是雙腳比較粗壯，翅膀比噪鶥屬的鳥類（Garrulax）短，和奇眉屬（Sibia，目前屬名 Heterophasia）的鳥類有一定程度的親緣關係。

---

1 譯註：全名Joseph Beal Steere，為美國鳥類學家，曾在亞馬遜流域、祕魯、安地斯、中國、福爾摩沙（臺灣）、菲律賓群島進行鳥類研究，著有《福爾摩沙及其住民：19世紀美國博物學家的台灣調查筆記（Formosa and Its Inhabitants）》（前衛出版，2009）。

黃胸藪眉（Liocichla steerii），新種，圖14。
全身羽色為橄欖綠色；頭冠和枕部、下頷和喉嚨、脇部和腰部呈現煙灰色；眼睛前方一角有橘黃色斑點；頸後和下腹部羽毛有黃色條紋，末端大部分為橘色；胸部、腹部和翅膀邊緣為黃色。腋下為煙灰色；鳥喙和雙腳為淡褐色；尾羽為橄欖綠；尾羽末端為白色，呈方形；四根中央尾羽末端為白色，末端前帶有一條黑色條紋；兩側的三根尾羽的外羽瓣末端亦為黑色；次級飛羽為栗色，內羽瓣和前半部皆為黑色，每根羽毛末端都呈白色，外羽瓣為黃綠色，羽軸是黑色。體長7.5英吋、翼長2.7英吋、尾長3.4英吋。2
