福爾摩沙島植物誌[4]
這份名錄所列的植物，大部分都是由我自己採集的，但並非全部。名錄中有些植物並不是我採集到的，而是之前賈祿先生（Chas）寄給倫敦皇家植物園[5]標本館的物種。威爾佛（Charles Wilford）是倫敦皇家植物園的蒐藏經理，他隨著皇家艦隊「不屈號」和我一起探勘福爾摩沙島上的部分地區。我將我帶回來的所有植物都寄給了威廉．虎克先生（William Hooker），並都將保存在公立標本館，他好心地將下列由標本館館長布列克先生（Alan Black）所擬定的植物名錄提供給我。可惜的是，有些標本受損過於嚴重，或是太不完整以至於無法辨識。標註有星號的13種植物[6]，威廉爵士（Sir William）注意到它們「可能是新種」。我們可以看到，在名錄中，最廣為人知的福爾摩沙植物「蓪草（Rice-paper Plant, Aralia papyrifer，目前學名 Tetrapanax papyriferus）」消失了。這不是因為它很稀有，在福爾摩沙島北部淡水（Tamsuy）的丘陵地上四處都長有蓪草。在這裡我要提醒一下，名錄中的植物沒有一種是採自於海拔3,000英呎以上。

LIST OF PLANTS
FROM THE
ISLAND OF FORMOSA, OR TAIWAN.
—————————
The majority of these Plants were collected by myself, but not all. Some included in the List I did not procure, but they had been previously sent to the Kew Herbarium by Mr. Chas. Wilford, Collector for the Royal Gardens of Kew, who explored parts of the Island in company with myself, in 1857, in H.M.S. “Inflexible.” Sir William Hooker, to whom I sent all the plants I brought home, to be deposited in the Public Herbarium, has kindly supplied me with the following list of names, drawn up by Mr. Black, Curator of the Herbarium. Some of the specimens unfortunately were too damaged, and others were too imperfect for identification. The 13 marked with asterisks Sir William observes, are “seemingly new species.” It will be seen that the name of the best known Formosan plant, the Aralia papyrifera (Rice-paper plant) is missing. This is not owing to its rarity; for about the North end of Formosa, and about Tamsuy it grows abundantly on the hill sides. I may here remark that none of these plants were gathered at a higher altitude than 3,000 feet.
ROBERT SWINHOE.
